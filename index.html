Your guide

About Tom Fallowfield

After graduating philosophy from Durham, Tom spent 25 fairly unhappy years as a generalist web designer, doing what clients told him to, and building websites which looked… like websites.

Then came a blinding realisation: we have been doing this wrong! Pretty design is not the central issue. Most small businesses exist with very little thought given to the most important question of all, why on earth should anyone buy their stuff?

Vanilla Killer came into being to fix this, by helping businesses step outside their own thinking and construct a clear, logical argument for why they deserve to win.

These days Tom can be found on LinkedIn talking endlessly about how to improve your messaging.
