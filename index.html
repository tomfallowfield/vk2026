Privacy policy

Who we are
Vanilla Killer (“we”, “us”) operates this website and uses the data you provide to respond to enquiries, run our business, and improve the site. Contact: tom@vanillakiller.com.

Data we collect

  • Form submissions. When you submit the “Book a call”, “Website review”, or lead-magnet forms we receive the fields you fill in (e.g. name, email, website, message). We use this to respond to you and to manage enquiries.
  • Optional cookie. If you accept our cookie banner, we set a cookie that stores a visitor ID, visit count, referrer information, which buttons you clicked and which forms you submitted, and (if you watch videos) how far you watched. This helps us understand how the site is used and improve conversions. The cookie is stored for up to 365 days. You can decline the cookie; the site still works and we do not set this tracking cookie.
  • Server logs. When you submit a form, we log a timestamp, your IP address, and browser user-agent on our server for security and debugging. We do not use these for marketing.
  • Google Analytics. We use Google Analytics to see how the site is used (e.g. pages visited, device and browser type, approximate location). This involves cookies and similar technologies. Google processes this data according to their Privacy Policy.

How we use your data
We use form data to reply to you, to add you to our CRM (Notion) and, where relevant, to our email list (Mailchimp) so we can send the content you asked for (e.g. lead magnets) or occasional follow-ups. We use the optional cookie data and Google Analytics to analyse site usage and improve the experience. We do not sell your data.

Who we share it with
Form data is processed by our own server and sent to Notion (CRM) and Mailchimp (email). We also use Google Analytics; Google receives and processes analytics data as described in their privacy policy. These providers may store data outside the UK/EEA. We use them under their standard terms and privacy policies.

Your rights
You can ask us for a copy of the personal data we hold about you, to correct it, or to delete it. You can object to our use of your data or withdraw consent (e.g. for cookies – use your browser settings to clear cookies after declining). To exercise any of this, email tom@vanillakiller.com. You also have the right to complain to a supervisory authority.
